About HackSRM

Welcome to HackSRM! HackSRM is a premium student-led hackathon with participants all across the country. This aims to bring together some of the smartest students under one roof to provide technical solutions to potential real-world problems. HackSRM is a highly competitive hackathon and is meant for students who have flair for technology and believe in doing social good, but wait, under 24 hours. HackSRM aims to be a great platform for (smart) young minds to showcase their potential, and do their bit help build a greater tomorrow. Come, join us for our first technical extravaganza with well-known guest speakers, great internship opportunities, attend workshops, an option to network with like-minded people and learn, and of course, some great hacks to show in the end.

Why HackSRM

The winner gets a cash prize of ₹ 1,00,000. There are exciting prizes for runners-up and special sponsor company prizes to be won. If this is your first hackathon, we’ll ensure this would be a great learning experience. The hackathon is free to attend and open to all students currently enrolled in a technical undergraduate course. With exciting goodies to take back, you are destined to have mindful of a pure genius. Amazing food to keep the mind working well, is on us. 24 hours of absolute madness, we assure you an enriching and memorable experience at HackSRM.

So join us on this path of epicness!

About The Organizers


Organized by the students who have consistently conquered national and international hackathons like HackMIT, HackIIITD, Hack Harvard, Playscape@Berkeley, HINT, IGDC by Kamk University, Robotics Challenge at IIT Roorkee and countless more, are putting the ingredients together to conduct this one of its kind event. Institutes like QS Silver Awardee, Next Tech Lab, the ACM Student Chapter and Ennovab: Entrepreneurial Innovation Lab, powered by SCET, University of California, Berkeley are also actively involved to realize the largest hackathon in the country.
